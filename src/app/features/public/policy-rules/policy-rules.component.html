Policy Rules & Guidelines

Understand the terms that protect you. Transparency is our priority.

1. Enrollment & Eligibility

Policies are available for individuals aged 18 to 65. Children can be added as dependents from age 3 months onwards.

2. Waiting Periods

A standard waiting period of 30 days applies for all non-accidental claims. Pre-existing diseases have a waiting period of 2 years.

3. Claim Submission Process

  • Notify us within 24 hours of hospitalization.
  • Submit original documents within 15 days of discharge.
  • Claims are processed within 7 working days of receipt of all documents.
